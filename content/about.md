My name is Luigi Marini. I live in Oxford, where I work as an [academic](https://luigi-marini.github.io/). Here you can find some photographs I make as a hobby.

I have eclectic tastes within photography, but I am particularly interested in a tradition of early to late 20th century artistic and documentary work, which is socially and politically engaged, humanist, but also formal, modernist, industrial, architectural. Chris Killip, Paul Strand, Josef Koudelka, August Sander, Ernst and Hilla Becher, to name just a few, are among my favourite artists in the medium.

I am open to questions, requests and commissions. For any comments or enquiries, you can [contact me by email](/contact/).
